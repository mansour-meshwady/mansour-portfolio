Customer Churn Analysis

Tools: Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Analyzed over 45,000 customer records to evaluate marketing campaign performance, identify high-converting customer segments, and uncover factors influencing term deposit subscriptions. Delivered recommendations to improve campaign effectiveness and targeting.

Tools: Python, Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Business Outcome: Provided data-driven insights into consumer habits and wellness trends, helping stakeholders better understand behavioral patterns associated with coffee consumption.
